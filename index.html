Our Mission

At KropWise, we believe that every farmer deserves access to affordable, accurate agricultural advice. Our mission is to democratize agricultural expertise through artificial intelligence, making professional-grade crop analysis available to farmers of all scales.

We're committed to sustainable farming practices and helping farmers maximize their yields while minimizing environmental impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KropWise

Our AI models currently achieve an accuracy rate of 92-95% for common crop diseases in our supported regions. Accuracy varies by specific disease and crop type. We continuously improve our models as we gather more data from farmers across different regions.

Basic crop analysis is completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core disease detection and recommendations will always remain free to ensure accessibility for all farmers.

We currently support major crops including maize, wheat, soybeans, potatoes, tomatoes, citrus fruits, and grapes. We're constantly adding new crops based on farmer demand and regional importance.

For optimal results: 1) Take photos in natural daylight, 2) Focus on affected leaves or areas, 3) Include both close-up and whole-plant shots if possible, 4) Avoid shadows covering the plant, 5) Capture multiple angles if the issue isn't clear.

Currently, you need an internet connection to upload and analyze photos. However, we're developing offline functionality that will allow basic features to work without connectivity, scheduled for release in our next major update.
